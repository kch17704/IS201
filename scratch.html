My YouTube Channel

There are quite a few things that I enjoy doing in my free time, and one of these things is running my YouTube channel.

Ever since I was about twelve years old, I knew that I wanted to be a YouTuber. One of the kids in my class had his own channel, and I thought it was very cool that it was something you could just do. Because of that, a couple years ago, I making my own YouTube videos about a video game series called Five Nights at Freddy's.

Since then, I've gained over 100k subscribers and now make enough monthly that I can do YouTube as my primary job.
